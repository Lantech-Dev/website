What is Lantech Games?

Lantech Games is a service for people of any age or ability to make or play games.

How can I get Lantech Games?

Currently Lantech Games is still under development. But, we urgently need games made by the community, so if you have any please upload your game here.

How can I upload my game and what are the requirements?

It's really easy to upload your game to Lantech, simply fill out the google form. The requirements are as follows:

Your game must be either a .html file or .sb3 (scratch/turbowarp/penguinmod)file. Your game must also follow the Developer guidelines or we can't allow your game. Head to the Developer tools for more info.

What age do I have to be to use Lantech Games?

Anyone can use Lantech Games regardless of their age! Lantech Games is a community with a wide range of ages. However, if you are a parent and you are concerned about your child's online safety, there are a variety of tools in the app to limit games that your child sees based on age. This is only a recommendation; there is no legal requirement to limit games above a certain age group as all games should be suitable for children. Read the privacy policy for more info.
